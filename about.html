About Auto Group

Auto Group is a web application designed to help users easily create random groups from a list of names. Whether you're organizing teams for a project, planning group activities, or just looking to mix things up, Auto Group simplifies the process of group formation.

Features

  • Input a list of names and specify the desired group size.
  • Randomly generates groups to ensure fairness and variety.
  • Option to download the generated groups as a PDF for easy sharing.
  • User-friendly interface for quick and efficient group creation.

How It Works

Simply enter the names of participants into the input field, specify how many members you want in each group, and click the "Generate Groups" button. The application will shuffle the names and divide them into groups based on your specified size. You can then review the groups and download them as a PDF if needed.
